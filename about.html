Our Services

Interior Design

At Leroy Architecture we provide interior design. We want to help you pick out the perfect furniture for your home. We do this by showing you the floor plan and measurements so you are able to find the right furniture.

Schematic Design

With schematic design we are able to show you the layout of your house by using 2D and 3D drawings of your soon to be home. You are going to be able to get a "looking down" persepective of yout house and a lanscape perspective.

3D Renderings

With this we are able to give you a deeper look into your new home. We are able to do this by planning out a 3D version of house on a computer. We highly recommend this service because you can get better detail on your future house.

Our Process

First, we set up a meeting at anyplace you want or we can do it online. We talk about what you are interested in, and if we are able to do your dream project we will gladly help you. Once we have out meeting we will start planning. We will set up another meeting to talk about where you plan the location to be and what you expect within the house. We will then start the blueprint. Once we the blueprint is all set up, we will set up another meeting. This meeting is for you to geet your opinion on the blueprint, and once it has edits and your approval we will then start on a 3D overview. This will help you get a better understanding of your building. If you would like we can have another meeting with a local construction worker, and get their perspective. Once everthing has been approved by you, we can get started on your lovely new home!
