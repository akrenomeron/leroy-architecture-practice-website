Our Projects

Tindog Buraren

Tindog Buraren is girl's private high school that is located in Leyte, Phillipines. It is a small building mainly because it has only two classrooms. It has wood for part of the sides, and a slanted roof.

18-Units Apartment Complex

This apartment complex is located in Lodi, California. It is an 18 unit apartment with a cafe at the bottom. The outer walls are made of a combination of redwood, gray concrete, and white concrete.

Simon Place Dementia Facility

In our projects page you will find two Simon Place facilities. This project is the Simon Place Dementia Facility located in Manila, Phillipines. It has over 60 rooms and over four stories.

Blues Alley Studio Apartments

Blues Alley Studio Apartments is an apartment complex with three stories, with the top story having a pool. It is located in Roxas City in the Phillipines. There are over 100 rooms in this complex.

Manila Residence

This is a house located in Manila, Phillipines with an all white exterior. It is a two story house with 3 bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms and a pool in the backyard.

Sharif Plaza Complex

This complex is located in Downtown Sacramento. This complex is not just one store complex, it has space for about seven stores. An has an exterior of beige concrete.

Simon Place Senior Living

This another one of the facilities for Simon Place. This is a Senior Living place located in Manila, Phillipines. This complex has over 30 rooms and has two stories.
